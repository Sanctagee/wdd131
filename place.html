Heart of Europe

Germany blends rich history with modern innovation, from medieval castles to cutting-edge automotive technology. The country is famous for its Oktoberfest celebrations, Christmas markets, and contributions to classical music through composers like Beethoven and Bach.

With 16 distinct federal states, Germany offers diverse landscapes including the Bavarian Alps, Black Forest, and Baltic Sea coastlines.

Cultural Highlights

Oktoberfest: The World's Largest Volksfest

Every September-October, Munich transforms for the legendary Oktoberfest, a 16-18 day festival attracting over 6 million visitors. Originating in 1810 to celebrate a royal wedding, it's now a global symbol of Bavarian culture.
