AustraFavy's Cakes and Events

A Website Planning Document

Site Name

AustraFavy's Cakes and Events

This name reflects the actual brand name of a local business that specializes in cake baking and event services. It aligns with her logo and business identity.

Optional domain: favyscakesandevents.com

Site Purpose

The website will:

  • Showcase custom cakes, snacks (samosas, doughnuts), and event planning services.
  • Allow customers to place orders via an online booking form (JavaScript-powered).
  • Display a gallery of past work and pricing information.
  • Provide contact details (phone: 09169822417) and social media links.

Scenarios (Target Audience Questions)

  1. How do I order a custom cake for a wedding?
  2. What snack options are available for a birthday party?
  3. How can I order a birthday cake and get it delivered to my location?
  4. What are your prices and delivery options for wedding cakes?
  5. Do you have a gallery where I can see previous cake designs?
  6. What packages are available for event planning and bridal shower decorations?
